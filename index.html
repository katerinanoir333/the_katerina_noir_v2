House Rules

Before requesting attention, understand the etiquette.

Introduce yourself with confidence, clarity, and respect. Empty messages and vague requests are ignored.

No verification, no access. Sensitive details should not be sent casually; private instructions are shared only after screening.

A thoughtful tribute is a signal of seriousness. It is not a purchase of approval, intimacy, physical contact, or any specific outcome.

Privacy, manners, emotional intelligence, and calm communication are expected. The House protects its peace.

False information, pressure, surprise guests, or boundary testing mean immediate disqualification.
